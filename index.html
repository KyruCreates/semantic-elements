What are semantic elements?

Semantic elements clearly define its content to both the developer and the browser. Some examples are found throughout this page and shown below, but some common ones are [header] and [footer]. Previously, [div] or [span] were used to define elements. They are examples of non-semantic elements - they tell us nothing about their content.
